Moor up in one of the beautiful harbours you will pass on your cruise.  Delight in the bars and restaurants where you can listen to local folklore and be entertained by traditional song.  Take in the atmosphere before you move on to your next port of call.

Text for Island slide

Moor your boat in the clear blue sea off one of the many secluded islands you will come across.  Dive in and swim to shore where you can sunbathe or explore.  While away the hours relaxing in tranquil surroundings before cruising into the sunset.
